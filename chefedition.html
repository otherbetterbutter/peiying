I Am Talented

Chef Edition — Publication

Designed for the I Am Talented programme Culinary Arts Workshop, Chef Edition is a reflection booklet that helps young students discover healthy cooking—and imagine where a future in food could take them.

Why This Project?
Workshops come and go—but what sticks with students afterward? Chef Edition was my way of making the experience last. Designed for students from disadvantaged backgrounds, the booklet helps them document not just what they did, but how they felt and what they learned—through recipes, journal prompts, and moments of joy. This project allowed me to bridge design with education and social impact. I wanted the booklet to feel like a companion—something playful and approachable, yet powerful in guiding young minds toward their potential in culinary arts and beyond.

OUTPUTS

PROCESS

From brief to booklet

As a member of the IAT team, I led the concept development of Chef Edition, working closely with a friend to brainstorm ideas and execute the visual layout. We aimed to create more than just a handout—it had to feel personal, engaging, and purposeful for every student.

The process was collaborative and iterative. We refined the content through multiple rounds of feedback from both the IAT team and external collaborators. Each revision helped us sharpen the balance between structure and creativity.
